## Philosophy

Technology is most valuable when it reduces friction, expands access, and gives people greater agency over capital, information, ownership, and opportunity.

I believe strong systems — technical or human — are built through transparency, shared ownership, continual learning, and clear communication.

The long-term opportunity is larger than faster software or better tooling.  
It is the creation of more open, accessible, and participatory systems capable of unlocking innovation at a global scale.
